About [CV]

I am currently a second-year PhD student at School of Computing, University of Utah, advised by Prof. Qingyao Ai.

I mainly work on information retrieval and recommender system using textual knowledge, and explainable machine learning.

I am also interested in research topics including product search, query understanding, passage retrieval, conversation, knowledge graph, contrastive learning and item-aspect fairness.

Open to Collaboration

I like to collaborate with different people on different projects. If you are also interested into aforementioned research topics, feel free to contact me through email :)
